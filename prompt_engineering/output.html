# One-Day Meal Plan for Early Morning 20km Run

Here is a one-day meal plan for an athlete preparing for and recovering from a 20km early morning run:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 2,800 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (120g), Fat (80g), Carbs (380g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Pre-Run Snack (5:30 AM):** Banana (120g) with white bread (40g) and honey (15g).
    * Protein: 3g, Fat: 1g, Carbs: 50g

* **Post-Run Recovery Meal (7:30 AM):** Greek yogurt (200g, plain low-fat) with granola (50g), fresh berries (100g), and seeds (15g pumpkin seeds).
    * Protein: 25g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 65g

* **Mid-Morning Snack (10:00 AM):** Orange juice (250g) with a vegetable frittata (2 eggs + 50g mushrooms + 50g spinach cooked in 10g olive oil).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 25g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM):** Chickpea and vegetable curry with chickpeas (150g cooked), onion (80g), tomato (100g), spinach (100g), coconut milk (100g, light), and brown rice (80g cooked).
    * Protein: 28g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 85g

* **Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM):** Whole wheat bread (60g) with hummus (40g) and sliced cucumber (100g) and tomato (80g).
    * Protein: 10g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 40g

* **Dinner (7:00 PM):** Baked tofu (180g) with sweet potato (150g) and steamed broccoli (200g) drizzled with sesame oil (10g).
    * Protein: 28g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 85g

* **Evening Snack (9:30 PM):** Low-fat cottage cheese (150g) with pineapple (80g).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 1g, Carbs: 25g

This meal plan prioritizes easily digestible carbohydrates before and immediately after the run, complete proteins for muscle recovery, and whole grains for sustained energy throughout the day—all while accommodating vegetarian and nut-free requirements.
